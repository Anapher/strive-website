Equipment

Easily use your smartphone, tablet or laptop as webcam - just by scanning a QR code!

Breakout Rooms

  • Not separated from conference, more like the Discord room system
  • Moderators can send announcements to all participants any time
  • A timer is always visible to everyone
  • Options can be changed on the fly
  • No audio reconnecting when switching rooms
  • Global chat, room chat and private chat available

Polls

Many configuration options, can by anonymous or public, answer can be final or changable. Supported Polls:
  • Single choice
  • Multiple choice
  • Number estimation
  • Tag cloud

Whiteboard

  • Easy to use and extremely fast
  • Elements can be selected, moved and deleted
  • Collaborative editing can be enabled
